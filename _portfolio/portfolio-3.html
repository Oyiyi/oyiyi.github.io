" collection: portfolio --- Our team was the winner of the “LLM Fine-tuning and RAG Hackathon” held at AGI House, Hillsborough, CA, on 01/13/2024.

We leverages LLMs and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to develop a specialized Chinese Medicine LLM, aimed at making ancient and modern medical knowledge widely accessible and addressing significant global demand. Key achievements include compiling a comprehensive dataset of 700 ancient Chinese medicine books, 1,600 herbal tea formulas, and 6,200 symptom-treatment samples from hospital records. We fine-tuned the Mistralai/Mistral-7B-Instruct-v0.2 model using hospital data and implemented RAG techniques for personalized herbal tea recommendations.

Teammates: Wei Wu, Qi Tang, Ankit Shah, Aastha Singh

Sponsor & Judges: Together.ai, Langchain, weights&biases
